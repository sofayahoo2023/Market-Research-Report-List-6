<p><p>The Acousto-Optic Modulator (AOM) market divides into two main types: Fiber-Coupled Acousto-Optic Modulators and Free-Space Acousto-Optic Modulators. Fiber-Coupled AOMs are designed to integrate with fiber optics, enabling efficient light modulation and signal processing in compact systems. In contrast, Free-Space AOMs operate in open air, suitable for applications requiring direct beam manipulation without fiber constraints. Both types are crucial in telecommunications, laser systems, and imaging technologies, catering to diverse industry needs with distinct advantages.</p></p>
