<p><ul><li>Negative Etch Resists</li><li>Thick Negative Resists</li><li>Negative Lift-Off Resists</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The negative photoresist market encompasses several types, each serving distinct applications. Negative etch resists harden upon exposure to light, allowing unexposed areas to be etched away, making them ideal for precision microfabrication. Thick negative resists are designed for substantial layer applications, providing enhanced structure and support in high-aspect-ratio designs. Negative lift-off resists enable the selective removal of metal films by developing the exposed areas, facilitating intricate patterning in electronic components. These variations cater to advancing technology in semiconductor and microelectronics industries.</p></p>
