<p><p>The motion sensor market for wearables primarily focuses on accelerometers and gyroscopes. Accelerometers measure acceleration forces, enabling devices to detect orientation, movement, and activity levels. They play a key role in fitness tracking and sleep monitoring. Gyroscopes, on the other hand, provide angular velocity data, which helps determine the rotation and position of the wearable. Together, these sensors enhance user experiences in health monitoring, gaming, navigation, and augmented reality, driving innovation and growth in the wearables market.</p></p>
