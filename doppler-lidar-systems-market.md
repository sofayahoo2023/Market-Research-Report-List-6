<p><ul><li>Pulse</li><li>Continuous Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Doppler LiDAR systems are categorized into two main types: Pulse and Continuous Wave (CW). Pulse LiDAR systems emit laser pulses at intervals, measuring the time taken for reflections to gauge distance and velocity, making them effective for long-range applications. Continuous Wave systems, on the other hand, emit a constant laser beam, utilizing frequency shifting to determine speed and direction of moving objects. Both types serve crucial roles in various sectors, including autonomous vehicles, environmental monitoring, and urban planning.</p></p>
