<p><strong>The Under-display Fingerprint Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Module</li><li>Optical Fingerprint Module</li><li>Ultrasonic Fingerprint Module Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The under-display fingerprint module market comprises three main types: capacitive, optical, and ultrasonic. Capacitive fingerprint modules use electric fields to capture fingerprint patterns, providing quick and accurate recognition. Optical modules utilize light to create a visual image of the fingerprint, often offering high-resolution scans. Ultrasonic modules emit sound waves to penetrate the skin's surface, capturing detailed 3D fingerprint data, which enhances security. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to varying consumer preferences and device requirements in the smartphone industry.</p></p>
