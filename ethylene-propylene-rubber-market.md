<p><p>The Ethylene Propylene Rubber market primarily consists of two types: Ethylene Propylene Monomer (EPM) and Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM). EPM is a type of synthetic rubber characterized by its excellent resistance to heat, oxidation, and weathering, making it suitable for applications in automotive and industrial products. EPDM, on the other hand, incorporates diene, enhancing its elasticity and durability, making it ideal for roofing, seals, and gaskets. Both types are valued for their performance and versatility in various industries.</p></p>
