<p><strong>The Mechanical Tappets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Tappet</li><li>Roller Tappet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical tappets market consists of two main types: flat tappets and roller tappets. Flat tappets feature a simple design that relies on a flat surface making direct contact with the camshaft, suitable for older engines. Roller tappets, however, utilize rollers to reduce friction and wear, enhancing performance and efficiency in modern engines. The choice between flat and roller tappets affects engine longevity, power output, and maintenance needs, making it crucial for automotive manufacturers to consider when designing engines.</p></p>
