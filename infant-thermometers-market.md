<p><p>The infant thermometers market features various types designed for accurate temperature measurement in babies. Digital thermometers provide quick readings, often suitable for oral, rectal, or axillary use. Ear thermometers utilize infrared technology to measure temperature in the ear canal, offering fast results and convenience. Forehead thermometers, or temporal artery thermometers, use infrared sensors to gauge body temperature by swiping across the forehead, making them non-invasive and easy to use. Each type caters to different needs, balancing accuracy and user-friendliness.</p></p>
