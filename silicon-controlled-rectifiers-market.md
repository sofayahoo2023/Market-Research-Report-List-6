<p><p>Silicon Controlled Rectifiers (SCRs) are available in two main market types - Unidirectional SCR and Bidirectional SCR. Unidirectional SCRs allow current flow in only one direction, making them ideal for applications where current should only flow in one direction. On the other hand, Bidirectional SCRs allow current flow in both directions, making them suitable for applications where current needs to flow in either direction. Both types of SCRs have their own unique applications and are widely used in various industries for controlling power and voltage in electronic circuits.</p></p>
