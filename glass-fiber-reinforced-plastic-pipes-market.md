<p><p>The glass fiber reinforced plastic (GFRP) pipes market is segmented primarily into three types: polyester, vinyl ester, and epoxy. Polyester pipes are lightweight and cost-effective, suitable for various applications but have moderate chemical resistance. Vinyl ester pipes offer improved chemical resistance and durability, making them ideal for aggressive environments. Epoxy pipes provide superior strength and resistance to corrosion, often used in specialized industrial applications. Each type caters to specific requirements, enhancing performance in diverse operational contexts.</p></p>
