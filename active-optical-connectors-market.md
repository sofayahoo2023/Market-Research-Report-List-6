<p><p>Active Optical Connectors are categorized into two main types: Simplex and Duplex. </p><p>Simplex Active Optical Connectors are designed for single-way communication, allowing data transmission in only one direction. They are commonly used in applications where data flow is unidirectional, such as in broadcast systems or monitoring equipment. </p><p>On the other hand, Duplex Active Optical Connectors support bidirectional communication, allowing data transmission in both directions simultaneously. They are widely used in networking and telecommunications systems where data needs to flow back and forth between devices.</p></p>
