<p><p>An adsorption wheel is an innovative technology used for the thermal regeneration of materials that adsorb specific environmental contaminants, such as volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and odors. This technology employs a rotary wheel composed of adsorbent material, which rotates through an air stream to capture pollutants and subsequently releases them when heated, regenerating the adsorbent for repeated use. The efficiency and sustainability of adsorption wheels make them attractive for various industrial applications, including air purification and energy recovery.</p><p>The Adsorption Wheel Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.6% during the forecast period, driven by increasing regulatory pressures to reduce air pollution and the growing demand for clean air solutions across industries. The rising awareness of environmental issues and the need for effective VOC control are fueling market growth.
