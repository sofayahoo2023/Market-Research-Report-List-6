<p><p>The arrhythmia management devices market encompasses several key types: pacemakers, defibrillators, and ablation devices. Pacemakers regulate heart rhythms by delivering electrical impulses, ensuring consistent heartbeat. Defibrillators can correct life-threatening arrhythmias through high-energy shocks, restoring normal rhythm. Ablation devices target and destroy abnormal heart tissue responsible for irregular rhythms using targeted energy. Together, these technologies play a vital role in diagnosing and treating arrhythmias, enhancing patient outcomes and quality of life in individuals with cardiovascular conditions.</p></p>
