<p><p>The traction converter market is primarily categorized into two types: propulsion converters and integrated compact converters. Propulsion converters are specialized systems designed to convert electrical energy for powering traction systems in electric vehicles, enhancing efficiency and performance. Integrated compact converters, on the other hand, combine multiple functionalities into a single unit, optimizing space and weight in various applications. Both types focus on advancing electric propulsion technology, contributing to the growth of sustainable transportation solutions in the automotive industry.</p></p>
