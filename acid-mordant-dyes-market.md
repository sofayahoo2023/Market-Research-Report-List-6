<p><p>The Acid Mordant Dyes Market focuses on two main types of metal complex dyes: 1:1 type and 1:2 type. The 1:1 type features a single metal ion coordinated with one dye molecule, providing vivid colors and good stability for textiles. In contrast, the 1:2 type consists of one metal ion bonded to two dye molecules, enhancing colorfastness and depth. Both types are widely used in the textile industry, especially for wool, silk, and nylon, due to their vibrant shades and durability.</p></p>
