<p><p>Cellulose Acetate Propionate (CAP) is categorized into two main types based on plasticizer content: High Plasticizer CAP and Low Plasticizer CAP. High Plasticizer CAP contains a higher proportion of plasticizers, enhancing flexibility and processing characteristics, making it suitable for applications like coatings and adhesives. Conversely, Low Plasticizer CAP has reduced plasticizer levels, providing greater rigidity and durability, ideal for rigid films and packaging materials. Both types serve distinct market needs, driving demand across various industries, including automotive, cosmetics, and packaging.</p></p>
