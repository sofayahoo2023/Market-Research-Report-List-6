<p><p>The auto tire market is divided into two main types: Original Equipment (OE) tires and Replacement tires. OE tires are manufactured specifically for new vehicles and are designed to meet the specifications set by vehicle manufacturers, ensuring optimal performance and safety. In contrast, Replacement tires are sold aftermarket and cater to consumers who need to replace worn or damaged tires on their vehicles.
