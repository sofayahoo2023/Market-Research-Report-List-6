<p><p>The handheld drug detectors market encompasses various technologies for rapid drug identification. Infrared (IR) spectrometers use light absorption patterns to analyze substances. Mass Spectrometry (MS) determines molecular composition and structure by measuring ionized particles. Ion Mobility Spectrometry (IMS) identifies compounds based on their mobility in an electric field. Gas Chromatography separates and analyzes volatile substances through vaporization. Raman Spectrometry employs laser light to detect molecular vibrations, offering detailed information about chemical bonds. Each method enhances drug detection capabilities in diverse settings.</p></p>
