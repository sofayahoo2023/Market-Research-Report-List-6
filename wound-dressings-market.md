<p><p>The wound dressings market consists of various types including films, foam dressings, collagens, alginates, hydrocolloids, hydrogels, and super absorbers. Film dressings provide a protective barrier while allowing moisture vapor permeability. Foam dressings are absorbent and cushioning. Collagen dressings promote healing by delivering proteins. Alginates, derived from seaweed, absorb exudate and form a gel. Hydrocolloids create a moist environment for healing. Hydrogels offer cooling properties and moisture retention, while super absorbers manage high levels of exudate effectively.</p></p>
