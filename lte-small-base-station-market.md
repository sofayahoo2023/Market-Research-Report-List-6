<p><strong>The LTE Small Base Station Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Time Division Duplex (TDD)</li><li>Frequency Division Duplex (FDD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LTE Small Base Station market includes two primary duplexing types: Time Division Duplex (TDD) and Frequency Division Duplex (FDD). TDD allows for dynamic allocation of time slots for uplink and downlink communications, making it ideal for asymmetric data traffic. In contrast, FDD uses separate frequency bands for uplink and downlink, ensuring continuous data flow and lower latency, particularly beneficial for symmetric data traffic. Both types cater to diverse deployment scenarios in urban and rural environments, enhancing network capacity and coverage.</p></p>
