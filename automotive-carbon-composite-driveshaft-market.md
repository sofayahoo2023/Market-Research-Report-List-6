<p><ul><li>Rigid Universal Joint</li><li>Flexible Universal Joint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive carbon composite driveshaft market includes two primary types: rigid universal joints and flexible universal joints. Rigid universal joints are designed for applications requiring a solid connection between the driveshaft and the drivetrain components, offering high strength and minimal deflection. In contrast, flexible universal joints accommodate angular misalignment, providing improved shock absorption and enhanced vehicle handling. Both types leverage lightweight carbon composite materials, which contribute to reduced vehicle weight, improved fuel efficiency, and increased performance in modern vehicles.</p></p>
