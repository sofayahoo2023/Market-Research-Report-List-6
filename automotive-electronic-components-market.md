<p><p>The Automotive Electronic Components Market includes **Active Components** and **Passive Components**. Active components, such as microcontrollers, sensors, and transistors, require power to function and actively control or amplify electrical signals, enabling advanced features like ADAS and infotainment systems. Passive components, like resistors, capacitors, and inductors, do not require external power and primarily manage electrical flow, ensuring stability and filtering in circuits. Both are essential for modern vehicles, supporting safety, efficiency, and connectivity in automotive electronics.</p></p>
