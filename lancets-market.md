<p><p>The lancet market is categorized into two primary types: safety lancets and homecare lancets. Safety lancets are designed for controlled and safe blood sampling, minimizing the risk of accidental needle sticks, and often feature mechanisms that retract or shield the needle. Homecare lancets, on the other hand, are intended for personal use, allowing individuals to perform blood glucose testing conveniently at home. Both types prioritize user safety and convenience, catering to the needs of patients and healthcare providers alike.</p></p>
