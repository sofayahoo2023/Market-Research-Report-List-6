<p><p>The steel cord belts market is primarily categorized into two types: strand steel cord conveyor belts and layer steel cord conveyor belts. Strand steel cord conveyor belts are characterized by their construction of tightly bundled steel wires, providing superior tensile strength and durability, ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, layer steel cord conveyor belts consist of multiple layers of steel cords, offering additional flexibility and stability, making them suitable for various transport needs across industries. Both types enhance efficiency and longevity in material handling.</p></p>
