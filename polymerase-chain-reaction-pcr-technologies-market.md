<p><p>The PCR technologies market encompasses various techniques for amplifying DNA. Digital PCR quantifies nucleic acids with high precision, while Real-Time Q-PCR enables monitoring of amplification in real-time. Standard PCR is the basic method for DNA amplification. Assembly PCR constructs long DNA sequences, and Inverse PCR facilitates the amplification of unknown flanking regions. Multiplex PCR allows simultaneous amplification of multiple targets, enhancing efficiency. Hot Start PCR improves specificity by activating enzymes only at high temperatures, minimizing non-specific amplification.</p></p>
