<p><p>Semi-Skimmed Fish Meal is available in two main market types: Flame Dried (FD) Fish Meal and Steam Dried (SD) Fish Meal. FD Fish Meal is produced by heating the fish meal to high temperatures using direct flame heating methods, resulting in a darker color and higher protein content. On the other hand, SD Fish Meal is made by steam drying the fish meal without direct contact with flames, leading to a lighter color and slightly lower protein content. Both types of fish meal are commonly used as protein-rich feed for animals.</p></p>
