<p><p>The polyester coatings market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based polyester coatings. Water-based polyester coatings use water as the primary solvent, resulting in lower volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions and increased environmental compliance, making them ideal for eco-friendly applications. In contrast, solvent-based polyester coatings utilize organic solvents, offering superior durability and adhesion properties suited for industrial applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and consumer goods, depending on performance and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
