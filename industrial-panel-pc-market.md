<p><p>The Industrial Panel PC market is primarily divided into two types: fan-enabled and fanless Panel PCs. Fan-enabled Panel PCs use internal fans to provide active cooling, making them suitable for high-performance applications in environments with substantial heat generation. In contrast, fanless Panel PCs rely on passive cooling mechanisms, prioritizing durability and maintenance-free operation, which is ideal for harsh conditions with dust, moisture, and temperature variations. Both types cater to specific industrial needs, balancing performance and reliability.</p></p>
