<p><p>Automotive-grade multilayer ceramic capacitors (MLCCs) are essential components designed to ensure reliability and performance in automotive electronics. The market is divided into two primary types: conductive epoxy and standard type MLCCs. Conductive epoxy MLCCs utilize a conductive adhesive for mounting, providing enhanced thermal and mechanical stability, which is crucial in automotive applications. Standard type MLCCs, on the other hand, are the conventional capacitors known for their compact size and high capacitance, commonly used in various electronic circuits within vehicles.</p></p>
