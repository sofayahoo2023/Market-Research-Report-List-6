<p><p>The Aesthetic Lift Threads market is categorized into two main types: absorbable and non-absorbent materials. Absorbable materials, made from substances like polylactic acid, dissolve over time, offering temporary lifting effects with natural integration into the body. In contrast, non-absorbent materials, often made from polypropylene, provide permanent support and require surgical removal. Both types cater to different patient needs and preferences, influencing their applicability in cosmetic procedures for facial rejuvenation and contouring.</p></p>
