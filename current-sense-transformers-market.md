<p><p>The Current Sense Transformers market includes primarily two types: mutual inductance measuring current transformers and protective current transformers. Mutual inductance measuring transformers are designed for accurate current measurement in various applications, allowing for efficient monitoring and control of electrical systems. In contrast, protective current transformers are utilized for safeguarding electrical equipment by detecting faults and ensuring proper circuit protection. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing electrical system reliability and safety across industrial, commercial, and residential sectors.</p></p>
