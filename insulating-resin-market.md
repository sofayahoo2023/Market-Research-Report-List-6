<p><p>Insulating resin is a specialized polymer material used to provide electrical insulation, thermal resistance, and mechanical protection in various applications, including electronics, automotive, aerospace, and energy sectors. It is commonly used in transformers, motors, generators, and printed circuit boards (PCBs) to prevent electrical leakage and ensure safe operation. Insulating resins are available in various forms, such as epoxy, polyester, polyurethane, and silicone, each offering unique properties like high dielectric strength, thermal stability, and chemical resistance.</p><p>The Insulating Resin Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.7% during the forecast period.
