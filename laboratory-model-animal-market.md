<p><p>The laboratory model animal market consists of three main types: homozygote, heterozygote, and genetically modified animals. Homozygote animals carry identical alleles for specific genes, making them ideal for studying gene function and disease models. Heterozygote animals possess different alleles, which helps in understanding genetic variation and its effects on phenotypes. Genetically modified animals are engineered to carry specific alterations, enabling researchers to investigate gene interactions, disease mechanisms, and potential therapies, thereby advancing biomedical research and drug development.</p></p>
