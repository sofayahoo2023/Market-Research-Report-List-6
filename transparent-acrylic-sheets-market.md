<p><p>The transparent acrylic sheets market primarily consists of two types: extruded acrylic sheets and cast acrylic sheets. Extruded acrylic sheets are produced through a continuous process, resulting in a uniform thickness and lower production costs, making them ideal for applications like signage and displays. In contrast, cast acrylic sheets are made by pouring molten acrylic into molds, offering superior optical clarity and durability, often used in high-end applications such as optical devices and high-performance glazing. Both types cater to distinct market needs.</p></p>
