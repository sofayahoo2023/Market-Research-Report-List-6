<p><p>Meltblown PP nonwoven fabric is a type of fabric produced through the meltblown process, which entails extruding molten polymer to form fine fibers that are subsequently collected on a moving belt. Due to its unique characteristics, meltblown PP nonwoven fabrics are widely used in applications such as face masks, medical textiles, filtration media, and other hygiene products.</p><p>The global meltblown PP nonwoven fabric market is poised for significant growth, driven by increasing demand in the healthcare sector, particularly post-pandemic, where the focus on personal protective equipment (PPE) has intensified.
