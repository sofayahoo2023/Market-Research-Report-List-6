Cloud-based warranty management solutions are hosted on the provider's servers, offering accessibility from anywhere, automatic updates, and reduced infrastructure costs. Conversely, on-premises software is installed directly on the user's hardware, providing greater control over data security and customization but typically requiring higher upfront investment and maintenance efforts.
