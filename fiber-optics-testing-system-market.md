<p><ul><li>Single Fiber Mode</li><li>Mutiple Fiber Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fiber Optics Testing System market is categorized into two main types: Single Fiber Mode and Multiple Fiber Mode. Single Fiber Mode focuses on the transmission of light through a single optical fiber, which allows for higher bandwidth and longer distances, making it ideal for telecommunications and high-speed networks. Conversely, Multiple Fiber Mode involves multiple fibers transmitting data simultaneously, useful for shorter distances and applications requiring higher data capacities, like data centers and local area networks. Both types play critical roles in modern data transmission.</p></p>
