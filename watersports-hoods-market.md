<p><p>The watersports hoods market is primarily segmented into neoprene and fleece hoods. Neoprene watersports hoods are designed for thermal insulation and moisture resistance, making them ideal for cold water activities such as surfing and diving. They provide a snug fit, preventing water entry and enhancing warmth. Fleece watersports hoods, on the other hand, offer lightweight comfort and excellent insulation for milder conditions. They are typically used for activities like kayaking or paddleboarding, prioritizing breathability and flexibility.</p></p>
