<p><strong>The Transcatheter Embolization Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Particle embolic</li><li>Coil embolic</li><li>Liquid embolic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transcatheter Embolization Device market comprises three primary types: particle embolics, coil embolics, and liquid embolics. Particle embolics are small particles designed to occlude blood vessels, effectively blocking blood flow to targeted areas. Coil embolics involve metal coils that induce clot formation, promoting occlusion in larger vessels. Liquid embolics are injectable substances that flow through blood vessels and solidify to create blockages. Each type serves distinct clinical needs in minimally invasive procedures aimed at treating vascular conditions.</p></p>
