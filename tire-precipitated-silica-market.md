<p><p>The tire precipitated silica market is primarily categorized into Highly Dispersible Silica (HDS) and Standard Silica (STS). HDS is designed for superior dispersion in rubber compounds, enhancing tire performance, durability, and fuel efficiency. It is often used in high-performance tires. In contrast, STS offers good reinforcement but may lack the advanced dispersion qualities of HDS. STS is typically utilized in standard tires where cost-effectiveness is a priority, balancing performance with economic considerations. Both types play distinct roles in tire manufacturing.</p></p>
