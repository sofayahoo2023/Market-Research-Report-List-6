<p><p>The Downhole Drilling Tools market comprises various types, primarily Fixed Cutter Drill Bits and Roller Cone Drill Bits. Both types cater to specific drilling needs, enhancing operational efficiency and effectiveness in exploration and extraction activities.</p></p>
