<p><ul><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Pressing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>NdFeB bonded magnets are classified into two primary market types: injection molding and pressing. Injection molding involves mixing powdered NdFeB with a polymer and then forming the magnets through a molding process, allowing for complex shapes and designs. In contrast, pressing involves compacting the magnet powder into molds using a high-pressure technique, typically resulting in stronger magnets but with simpler geometries. Both methods cater to diverse applications, offering options based on performance requirements and design flexibility.</p></p>
