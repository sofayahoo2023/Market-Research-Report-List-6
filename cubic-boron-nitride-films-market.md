Cubonite offers a blend of toughness and wear resistance, suitable for cutting tools and abrasives. Elbor, with its unique properties, is utilized in specialized applications like polishing and grinding. Together, these variants cater to diverse industrial needs, enhancing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
