<p><p>Fiber splice closures are crucial components in fiber optic networks, providing protection and organization for spliced optical fibers. The market is primarily segmented into two types: dome type and horizontal type. Dome type closures are typically cylindrical and designed for underground applications, offering robust environmental protection and accommodating a higher fiber count. In contrast, horizontal type closures are flatter and often used for above-ground installations, allowing for easier access and management of fiber connections. Each type serves specific deployment needs.</p></p>
