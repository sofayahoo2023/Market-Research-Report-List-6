<p><ul><li>Screw-based</li><li>Ratchet-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical pencils market is primarily categorized into screw-based and ratchet-based types. Screw-based mechanical pencils feature a mechanism where users twist the barrel to advance the lead, allowing for precise control and customization of lead length. In contrast, ratchet-based mechanical pencils employ a push-button mechanism that advances the lead incrementally with each click, providing quick and convenient lead feeding. These distinct mechanisms cater to different user preferences, enhancing their application in both writing and drawing tasks.</p></p>
