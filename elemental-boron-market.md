<p><strong>The Elemental Boron Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Amorphous Boron</li><li>Crystalline Boron</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elemental boron market is divided into two primary types: amorphous boron and crystalline boron. Amorphous boron is characterized by its irregular structure and is typically used in applications like pyrotechnics and as a dopant in semiconductor technology due to its high reactivity. Crystalline boron, on the other hand, has a well-defined structure, making it suitable for applications in advanced materials, abrasives, and as a neutron absorber in nuclear reactors, where stability and purity are essential.</p></p>
