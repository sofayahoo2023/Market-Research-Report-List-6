Category Management involves strategically grouping similar products or services to streamline procurement processes. Source Management focuses on identifying and managing suppliers to ensure quality and cost-effectiveness. Procurement Management deals with overall purchasing activities and vendor relationships. Supplier Management involves monitoring and evaluating supplier performance to drive continuous improvement in sourcing and procurement processes.</p></p>
