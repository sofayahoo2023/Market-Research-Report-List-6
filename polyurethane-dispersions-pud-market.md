<p><p>Polyurethane dispersions (PUD) are waterborne coatings and adhesives that provide excellent durability, flexibility, and resistance to moisture. The market features three main types: One-Component Polyurethane, which is ready-to-use and cures through moisture; Two-Component Polyurethane, which requires mixing of resin and hardener for curing; and Urethane-Modified products, which enhance performance by combining properties of PUD with other polymers. Each type serves diverse applications in industries like automotive, construction, and textiles, catering to specific performance needs.</p></p>
