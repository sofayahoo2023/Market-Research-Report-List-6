<p><p>Folding cartons packaging encompasses various types, primarily focusing on standard and aseptic cartons. Standard cartons are widely used for retail packaging and are made from lightweight paperboard, providing effective protection for products while allowing for vibrant graphics and branding. Aseptic cartons, on the other hand, are designed for food and beverage products that require extended shelf life without refrigeration. These cartons are manufactured in a sterile environment, preventing contamination and preserving freshness, making them ideal for liquid packaging solutions.</p></p>
