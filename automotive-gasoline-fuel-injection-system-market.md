<p><ul><li>Single-Point or Throttle Body Injection</li><li>Port or Multipoint Fuel Injection</li><li>Sequential Fuel Injection</li><li>Direct Injection</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive gasoline fuel injection system market comprises several types. Single-point or throttle body injection delivers fuel through a single nozzle, while port or multipoint fuel injection supplies fuel to each cylinder, enhancing efficiency. Sequential fuel injection delivers fuel precisely as needed, improving combustion. Direct injection injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber for better power and efficiency. Other types may include hybrid systems or emerging technologies, catering to varying performance needs and emission standards in modern vehicles.</p></p>
