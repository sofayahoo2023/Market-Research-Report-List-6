<p><strong>The Automotive Emission Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-dispersive Infrared (NDIR) Analyzers</li><li>Flame Ionization Detector (FID)</li><li>Chemiluminescence Analyzer (CLA)</li><li>Constant Volume Sampler (CVS)</li><li>Other Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive emission analyzer market includes several types of technologies for measuring exhaust emissions. Non-dispersive Infrared (NDIR) analyzers measure gases like CO2 and CO by detecting infrared light absorption. Flame Ionization Detectors (FID) specialize in analyzing hydrocarbons by ionizing samples in a flame. Chemiluminescence Analyzers (CLA) quantify nitrogen oxides through light emitted during chemical reactions. Constant Volume Samplers (CVS) collect exhaust samples under controlled conditions. Other types may include advanced sensors and hybrid technologies for comprehensive emissions analysis.</p></p>
