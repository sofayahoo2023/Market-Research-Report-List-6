<p><strong>The Biophotonics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Vivo</li><li>In-Vitro</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biophotonics encompasses two primary market types: in-vivo and in-vitro. In-vivo biophotonics refers to imaging and diagnostic techniques applied directly within living organisms, enabling real-time observation of biological processes. This approach is crucial for innovative therapies and personalized medicine. In contrast, in-vitro biophotonics involves studying biological samples outside their original context, such as cells or tissues in laboratory settings. This method is vital for research, drug development, and understanding disease mechanisms, facilitating advancements in healthcare solutions.</p></p>
