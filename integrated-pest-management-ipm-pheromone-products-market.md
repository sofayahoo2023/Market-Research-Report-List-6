Aggregation pheromones lure pests to specific areas for trapping or elimination. Oviposition deterring pheromones inhibit egg-laying by target species, reducing infestations. Alarm pheromones signal danger to pests, prompting avoidance and enhancing pest control efforts. These products support eco-friendly pest management strategies by utilizing natural chemical signals to disrupt pest behaviors.</p></p>
