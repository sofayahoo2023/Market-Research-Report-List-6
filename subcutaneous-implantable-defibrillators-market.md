<p><p>Subcutaneous Implantable Defibrillators (S-ICDs) are advanced medical devices designed to monitor and treat life-threatening arrhythmias, providing defibrillation therapy without the need for transvenous leads. They are implanted under the skin, typically in the left lateral chest area, and utilize an electrode placed subcutaneously to detect and treat abnormal heart rhythms.
