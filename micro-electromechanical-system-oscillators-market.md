<p><p>The Micro Electromechanical System (MEMS) oscillators market encompasses several types. Simple Packaged MEMS Oscillators (SPMO) offer basic functionality in compact sizes. Temperature-Compensated MEMS Oscillators (TCMO) provide stability across temperature variations. Voltage-Controlled MEMS Oscillators (VCMO) adjust frequency based on input voltage. Frequency Select MEMS Oscillators (FSMO) allow for predefined frequency outputs. Digitally Controlled MEMS Oscillators (DCMO) enable frequency tuning via digital signals. Spread-Spectrum MEMS Oscillators (SSMO) enhance signal integrity by distributing energy across a frequency range, reducing interference.</p></p>
