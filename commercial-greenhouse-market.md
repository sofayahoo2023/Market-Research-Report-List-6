<p><p>The commercial greenhouse market comprises primarily two types: glass greenhouses and plastic greenhouses. Glass greenhouses offer superior light transmission and durability, making them ideal for high-value crops and long-term investments. They provide better climate control but involve higher initial costs. Conversely, plastic greenhouses, typically made from polyethylene, are more cost-effective and easier to install, allowing for quick setup and versatility in growing conditions. Both types cater to various agricultural needs, enhancing productivity and yield in controlled environments.</p></p>
