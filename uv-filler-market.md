<p><strong>The UV Filler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1K UV Filler</li><li>2K UV Filler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV filler market is categorized into 1K and 2K formulations. 1K UV fillers are single-component products that do not require a hardener, making them easy to use and ideal for applications needing quick curing. In contrast, 2K UV fillers consist of two components: a base and a hardener. They offer enhanced durability, stronger adhesion, and better chemical resistance, making them suitable for demanding industrial applications. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, electronics, and construction, based on specific performance needs.</p></p>
