<p><ul><li>Wafer Tester</li><li>Packaged Device Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IC Tester market is divided into two main types: Wafer Testers and Packaged Device Testers. Wafer Testers evaluate semiconductor wafers during fabrication to ensure functionality before they are cut into individual chips. This testing is crucial for early defect detection, improving yield efficiency. Packaged Device Testers, on the other hand, assess complete integrated circuits after packaging, ensuring they meet performance specifications and reliability standards. Both types are essential for maintaining product quality and reliability in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
