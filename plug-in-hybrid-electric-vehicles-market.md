<p><p>Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) encompass two primary market types: Stored Electricity and On-Board Electric Generators. The Stored Electricity segment focuses on the use of external power sources to charge the vehicle's battery, enabling it to operate on electric power for shorter distances before transitioning to a combustion engine. Conversely, the On-Board Electric Generator market integrates an internal combustion engine that generates electricity to recharge the battery while driving, providing greater range and flexibility for longer trips. Both types enhance fuel efficiency and reduce emissions.</p></p>
