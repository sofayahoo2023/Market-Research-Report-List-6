<p><p>The Transcranial Doppler (TCD) market comprises two primary types: Implantable Transcranial Dopplers and Functional Transcranial Dopplers (fTCD). Implantable TCDs are devices surgically placed to provide continuous monitoring of cerebral blood flow, aiding in managing neurological conditions. In contrast, fTCD is a non-invasive method that assesses brain hemodynamics by measuring blood flow velocity in real-time, useful for both clinical and research applications. Together, these technologies enhance understanding of cerebral circulation and improve patient outcomes in neurological care.</p></p>
