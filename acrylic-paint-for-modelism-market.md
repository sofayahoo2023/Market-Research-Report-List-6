<p><p>Acrylic paint for modeling comes in three primary types: Heavy Body, Soft Body, and Fluid Paint. Heavy Body acrylics have a thick, buttery consistency, perfect for creating texture and impasto effects. Soft Body acrylics offer a smoother, more fluid application, ideal for fine details and blending. Fluid Paint, as the name suggests, is even thinner, allowing for easy layering and transparency, making it suitable for washes and glazing techniques in miniatures. Each type caters to different artistic techniques and effects in modelism.</p></p>
