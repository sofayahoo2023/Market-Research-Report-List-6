<p><p>The gas detection device market encompasses three primary types: detectors, transmitters, and controllers. Detectors identify the presence of hazardous gases, providing immediate alerts to safety personnel. Transmitters convert the detected gas levels into electrical signals, which are sent for further analysis or monitoring. Controllers manage the overall gas detection system, integrating inputs from detectors and transmitters to ensure proper response and safety measures are activated. Together, these components enhance safety in environments where gas exposure poses risks.</p></p>
