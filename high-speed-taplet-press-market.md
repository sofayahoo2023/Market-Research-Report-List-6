<p><ul><li>Single-punch</li><li>Rotary Tablet Presses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed tablet press market can be categorized into two main types: single-punch and rotary tablet presses. Single-punch presses operate by compressing powder into tablets using a single set of punches, suitable for small-scale production and laboratory use. In contrast, rotary tablet presses feature multiple stations on a rotating cam, allowing for a continuous, high-output production of tablets. This makes rotary presses ideal for large-scale manufacturing, offering efficiency and consistent tablet quality in pharmaceutical and nutraceutical applications.</p></p>
