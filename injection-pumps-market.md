<p><strong>The Injection Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Distributor Pump</li><li>Individual Control Pump</li><li>Unit Injection</li><li>Common Rail System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The injection pumps market is classified into four main types. Rotary distributor pumps deliver fuel to multiple cylinders simultaneously using a rotating mechanism. Individual control pumps provide precise fuel delivery to each cylinder, enhancing efficiency. Unit injection systems integrate the pump and injector for better performance and reduced emissions. Common rail systems distribute pressurized fuel to injectors, allowing for multiple injections per cycle and improved combustion control, making them popular in modern diesel engines.</p></p>
