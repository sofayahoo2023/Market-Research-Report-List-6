<p><p>Mineral-oil-based lubricants come in three main types based on their consistency: fluid, semi-fluid, and solid. Fluid lubricants have a low viscosity and flow easily, making them suitable for high-speed applications. Semi-fluid lubricants have a slightly higher viscosity and provide longer-lasting protection. Solid lubricants are in a semi-solid or paste form and are ideal for heavy-duty or extreme pressure environments. Each type of lubricant offers different benefits and is chosen based on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
