<p><p>Fluorosilicone gum is categorized into two primary market types: high-temperature vulcanization (HTV) and room temperature vulcanization (RTV). HTV fluorosilicone is designed for extreme thermal resistance, making it suitable for applications in aerospace and automotive sectors where heat exposure is critical. Conversely, RTV fluorosilicone cures at ambient temperatures, offering flexibility and ease of use for sealing and bonding in various industries. Both types exhibit excellent chemical resistance, contributing to their effectiveness in demanding environments.</p></p>
