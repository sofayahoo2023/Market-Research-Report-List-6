<p><p>The cardiovascular catheters market is divided into two main types: therapeutic catheters and diagnostic catheters. Therapeutic catheters are used for interventions such as angioplasty and stent placement, aimed at treating cardiovascular conditions by restoring blood flow. In contrast, diagnostic catheters are employed for diagnostic procedures like angiography, allowing clinicians to visualize the heart and blood vessels. Both types play critical roles in managing cardiovascular diseases, enhancing patient outcomes through minimally invasive techniques and accurate diagnosis.</p></p>
