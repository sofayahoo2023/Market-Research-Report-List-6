<p><ul><li>Polymer Matrix Composite(PMC)</li><li>Ceramic Matrix Composite(CMC)</li><li>Metal Matric Composite(MMC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SiC Fibres are used in various markets including PMC, CMC, and MMC. In Polymer Matrix Composites (PMC), SiC Fibres are incorporated into a polymer matrix to improve the mechanical and thermal properties of the composite. In Ceramic Matrix Composites (CMC), SiC Fibres are used to reinforce a ceramic matrix, providing high temperature resistance and durability. In Metal Matrix Composites (MMC), SiC Fibres enhance the strength and stiffness of the metal matrix, making it ideal for applications requiring high performance under extreme conditions.</p></p>
