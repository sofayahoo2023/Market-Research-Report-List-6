Methyl Cellulose, known for its unique gel-forming properties, finds applications in gluten-free and low-fat foods, contributing to moisture retention and improved mouthfeel. Both types are essential in the food industry for enhancing product quality and meeting consumer demands for healthier options.</p></p>
