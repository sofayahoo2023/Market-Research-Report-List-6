<p><p>The multimode optical fiber patch cord market consists of various types used for different applications. FC type fiber patch cords feature a screw-on connector for secure connections, ideal for high-vibration environments. SC type patch cords use a push-pull mechanism, ensuring easy connection and disconnection. ST type connectors are bayonet-style, suited for legacy networks. LC type patch cords are compact with a smaller form factor, facilitating high-density installations. Other types may include different connector styles or custom configurations for specialized needs.</p></p>
