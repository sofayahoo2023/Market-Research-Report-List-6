<p><ul><li>Prerenal Acute Kidney Injury</li><li>Intrinsic Renal Acute Kidney Injury</li><li>Postrenal Acute Kidney Injury</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Acute Kidney Injury (AKI) treatment and management market is categorized into three types: prerenal, intrinsic renal, and postrenal acute kidney injury. Prerenal AKI arises from reduced blood flow to the kidneys, requiring interventions like fluid resuscitation. Intrinsic renal AKI results from direct damage to kidney tissues, often treated with medications to manage the underlying cause. Postrenal AKI occurs due to urinary obstruction, necessitating procedures to relieve blockages. Each type demands tailored therapeutic approaches for effective management.</p></p>
