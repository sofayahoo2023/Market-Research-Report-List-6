<p><p>The transformers market is primarily divided into two categories: power transformers and distribution transformers. Power transformers are designed for high voltage applications, facilitating bulk electricity transmission over long distances, typically in utility and industrial sectors. Distribution transformers, on the other hand, step down voltage levels for local distribution, supplying electricity to residential and commercial consumers. Both types are essential for efficient power transmission and distribution, ensuring reliability and stability in electrical networks across various applications.</p></p>
