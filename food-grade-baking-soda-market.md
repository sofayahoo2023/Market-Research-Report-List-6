<p><p>The food grade baking soda market is primarily categorized by production methods. The Sodium Carbonate Method involves converting sodium carbonate into sodium bicarbonate through carbon dioxide absorption. The Trona Method extracts soda ash from the mineral trona, which is then processed into baking soda. Lastly, the Ammonium Carbonate Method utilizes ammonia and carbon dioxide in a chemical reaction to create sodium bicarbonate. Each method offers unique benefits in terms of purity, cost, and environmental impact, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
