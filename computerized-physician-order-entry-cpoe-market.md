<p><p>Computerized Physician Order Entry (CPOE) refers to electronic systems that allow healthcare providers to enter and manage medication orders, tests, and referrals digitally. The market comprises two types: standalone and integrated. Standalone CPOE systems operate independently, focusing solely on order entry without connecting to other health information systems, enhancing ease of use. In contrast, integrated CPOE systems are part of a comprehensive electronic health record (EHR), enabling seamless data exchange across various clinical functions, improving overall patient care and workflow efficiency.</p></p>
