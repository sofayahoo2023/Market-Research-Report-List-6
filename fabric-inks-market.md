<p><p>The fabric inks market comprises various types, each suited for specific applications. Reactive dye inks bond chemically with fibers, making them ideal for vibrant, long-lasting prints on natural fabrics. Acidic inks are designed for protein fibers like silk and wool, providing deep, rich colors and excellent wash fastness. Paint inks, on the other hand, are versatile and can be used on a wide range of materials, offering durability and opacity, making them suitable for both textiles and other surfaces.</p></p>
