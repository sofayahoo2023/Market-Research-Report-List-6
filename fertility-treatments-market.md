<p><p>The fertility treatments market includes various methods such as Intrauterine Insemination (IUI) and In Vitro Fertilization (IVF). IUI involves placing sperm directly into the uterus during ovulation, enhancing the chances of fertilization. It is less invasive and cost-effective compared to IVF. Conversely, IVF involves retrieving eggs and fertilizing them in a lab, followed by embryo transfer to the uterus. It is a more complex procedure, typically used for severe infertility cases, providing higher success rates but at greater expense.</p></p>
