<p><ul><li>Single Base Gunpowder</li><li>Double Base Gunpowder</li><li>Three Base Gunpowder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smokeless gunpowder market is typically categorized into three types: single base, double base, and three base gunpowder. Single base gunpowder, primarily composed of nitrocellulose, is known for its low smoke production and is used in various firearms. Double base gunpowder incorporates both nitrocellulose and nitroglycerin, offering higher energy and faster combustion, making it suitable for high-performance ammunition. Three base gunpowder combines multiple nitrated compounds, providing enhanced stability and performance, often utilized in specialized military applications.</p></p>
