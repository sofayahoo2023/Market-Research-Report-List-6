<p><p>The DNA purification market encompasses various types, each catering to different needs. Alcohol purification relies on precipitation methods to isolate DNA, while silica gel column-based purification leverages selective binding for efficient extraction. Agarose gel-based purification utilizes gel electrophoresis to separate and purify DNA fragments. Automated DNA purification employs machine technology for high-throughput processing and consistent results. Chemical-based purification involves the use of specialized reagents to enhance yield and purity. Each method offers distinct advantages depending on the application requirements.</p></p>
