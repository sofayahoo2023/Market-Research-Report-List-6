<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Custom LASIK Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wavefront Guided LASIK</li><li>Wavefront Optimized LASIK</li><li>Topography Guided LASIK</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Custom LASIK market encompasses advanced refractive surgery techniques tailored to individual eye characteristics. Wavefront Guided LASIK utilizes detailed wavefront mapping to create a personalized treatment plan, addressing higher-order aberrations for enhanced vision quality. Wavefront Optimized LASIK adjusts the laser profile to maintain natural corneal shape, minimizing potential visual distortions. Topography Guided LASIK combines corneal topography with wavefront data, offering precision corrections based on the eye's unique surface irregularities, resulting in enhanced visual outcomes.</p></p>
