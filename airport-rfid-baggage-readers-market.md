<p><p>The airport RFID baggage readers market is segmented into two main types: fixed and portable. Fixed type readers are strategically installed at various points within airport infrastructure, such as conveyor belts and entry/exit points, providing continuous tracking and monitoring of baggage throughout the airport. In contrast, portable type readers are handheld devices used by ground staff for on-the-spot scanning and tracking, offering flexibility and mobility. Both types enhance operational efficiency, reduce lost luggage incidents, and improve overall passenger experience.</p></p>
