<p><p>The Interventional X-Ray System market is categorized into two main types: Single Plane and Bi-plane Interventional X-Ray Systems. Single Plane systems utilize a single X-ray source and detector, ideal for straightforward procedures and offering cost-effectiveness. In contrast, Bi-plane systems feature two X-ray sources and detectors, providing comprehensive imaging from two angles simultaneously. This advanced capability enhances visualization during complex interventions, improving accuracy and patient outcomes. Both systems play critical roles in minimally invasive procedures across various medical fields.</p></p>
