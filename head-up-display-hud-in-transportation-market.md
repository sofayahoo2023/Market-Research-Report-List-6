<p><strong>The Head-Up Display (HUD) in Transportation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Windshield-Projected HUDs</li><li>Combiner-Projected HUDs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Head-Up Displays (HUD) in transportation enhance driver safety and convenience by projecting essential information onto the vehicle's windshield or a separate combiner. Windshield-Projected HUDs display data directly onto the windshield, allowing drivers to view speed, navigation, and alerts without looking away from the road. In contrast, Combiner-Projected HUDs use a small transparent screen mounted within the driver's line of sight, reflecting information from the dashboard. Both types aim to minimize distractions and improve situational awareness for a safer driving experience.</p></p>
