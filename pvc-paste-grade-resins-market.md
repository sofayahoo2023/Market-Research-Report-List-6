<p><strong>The PVC Paste Grade Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Micro Suspension Method</li><li>Emulsion Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVC paste grade resins are produced using two primary methods: the micro suspension method and the emulsion method. The micro suspension method involves dispersing PVC particles in a liquid medium, resulting in fine, uniform paste suitable for applications like coatings and adhesives. In contrast, the emulsion method combines PVC with surfactants in an aqueous solution, yielding a stable paste with excellent stability and flexibility. Both methods cater to diverse end-use sectors, including construction, automotive, and packaging industries.</p></p>
