<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-slip coatings market can be categorized into two primary types: water-based and solvent-based coatings. Water-based anti-slip coatings are eco-friendly, low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and easy to clean, making them suitable for indoor applications. In contrast, solvent-based coatings offer strong adhesion and durability, ideal for outdoor and high-traffic areas, though they contain higher VOC levels. Both types effectively enhance surface traction, ensuring safety across various environments, from industrial floors to residential spaces.</p></p>
