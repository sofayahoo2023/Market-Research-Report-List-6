<p><p>Molten Salt Technology Market primarily consists of four types: Parabolic Trough Systems, Power Tower Systems, Dish/Engine Systems, and Others. Parabolic Trough Systems use curved mirrors to focus sunlight onto a receiver tube filled with molten salt. Power Tower Systems use a centralized tower to collect and store molten salt. Dish/Engine Systems involve parabolic dishes that concentrate sunlight onto a receiver to produce heat. Other systems may include molten salt storage facilities or hybrid technologies combining different solar energy collection methods.</p></p>
