<p><strong>The X-ray Particle Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Diffraction Type</li><li>X-ray Fluorescence Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-ray Particle Analyzer market comprises two primary types: X-ray Diffraction (XRD) and X-ray Fluorescence (XRF). XRD is used to determine crystalline structure by measuring the angles and intensities of diffracted X-rays, making it ideal for material characterization. In contrast, XRF analyzes elemental composition by detecting the fluorescence emitted from a sample when excited by X-rays, which is valuable for applications in metallurgy, environmental science, and forensic analysis. Together, they serve diverse industries, enhancing material analysis and quality control.</p></p>
