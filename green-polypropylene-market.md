<p><strong>The Green Polypropylene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotactic Polypropylene</li><li>Atactic Polypropylene</li><li>Syndiotactic Polypropylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The green polypropylene market comprises three main types: isotactic polypropylene, atactic polypropylene, and syndiotactic polypropylene. Isotactic polypropylene is highly crystalline and offers excellent strength and heat resistance, making it suitable for various applications. Atactic polypropylene is amorphous and rubber-like, commonly used in adhesives and sealants due to its flexibility. Syndiotactic polypropylene features a regular alternating structure, providing a balance of strength and impact resistance, ideal for packaging and automotive parts. Together, they contribute to sustainable material solutions.</p></p>
