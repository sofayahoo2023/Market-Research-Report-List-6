<p><p>The gynecology operating tables market features two primary types: adjustable-height and fixed-height operating tables. Adjustable-height tables offer versatility, allowing healthcare providers to customize the table's height for optimal access and comfort during procedures. They enhance ergonomic conditions for surgeons and improve patient safety. In contrast, fixed-height tables maintain a consistent elevation, which may be suitable for specific procedures but lacks the flexibility of adjustable models. The choice between them often depends on the procedure's requirements and the facility's needs.</p></p>
