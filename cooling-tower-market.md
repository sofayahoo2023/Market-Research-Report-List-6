<p><strong>The Cooling Tower Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Evaporative Cooling Tower</li><li>Dry Cooling Tower</li><li>Hybrid Cooling Tower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cooling tower market comprises three main types: evaporative, dry, and hybrid cooling towers. Evaporative cooling towers use water evaporation to dissipate heat, making them highly efficient in warm climates. Dry cooling towers rely on air to remove heat without water usage, ideal for regions with scarce water resources. Hybrid cooling towers combine both methods, providing flexibility and efficiency by utilizing water and air in different operational modes, thus catering to various industrial needs while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
