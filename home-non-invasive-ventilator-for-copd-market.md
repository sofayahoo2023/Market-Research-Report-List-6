<p><p>The Home Non-Invasive Ventilator market for Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD) includes two primary types: Single Level Ventilators and Bilevel Ventilators. Single Level Ventilators provide consistent airflow at a fixed pressure, beneficial for patients with stable breathing patterns. In contrast, Bilevel Ventilators adjust the pressure for inhalation and exhalation, offering more comfort and support for patients with fluctuating respiratory needs. Both types enhance patient quality of life by facilitating easier breathing and reducing hospital visits.</p></p>
