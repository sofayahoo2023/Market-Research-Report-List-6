<p><p>The Automotive Armrest Market is categorized into two main types: OEM and Aftermarket. OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) armrests are designed and produced by the vehicle manufacturers, ensuring compatibility and integration with new vehicles. They are typically installed during the vehicle assembly process. In contrast, Aftermarket armrests are produced by third-party companies and are available for purchase after a vehicle's sale. Aftermarket options often offer customization and upgrades, allowing consumers to enhance comfort and functionality in existing vehicles.</p></p>
