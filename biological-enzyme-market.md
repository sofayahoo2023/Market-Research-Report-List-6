<p><p>The biological enzyme market comprises various types, each serving specific functions. Acid reductases facilitate the reduction of acids, while transferases are involved in transferring functional groups between molecules. Hydrolytic enzymes catalyze the breakdown of compounds through hydrolysis. Isomerases facilitate the rearrangement of molecular structures, and synthetases are responsible for synthesizing complex molecules from simpler ones. Together, these enzymes play critical roles in metabolic processes, industrial applications, and biotechnological innovations, driving demand across multiple sectors.</p></p>
