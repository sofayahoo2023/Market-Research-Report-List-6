RapidFlo emphasizes quick and efficient administration of anesthesia, ideal for fast-paced procedures. TurboFlo enhances the speed and adaptability of anesthesia delivery, allowing for rapid changes in dosage based on patient response. Together, these systems improve efficiency, patient safety, and outcomes in anesthetic care during surgical procedures.</p></p>
