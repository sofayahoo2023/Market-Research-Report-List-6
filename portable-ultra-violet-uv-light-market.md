<p><p>The Portable Ultra Violet (UV) Light market is categorized into three main types: UVA, UVB, and UVC lights. UVA lights primarily emit long wavelengths, used for curing plastics and in phototherapy. UVB lights generate medium wavelengths, important for vitamin D synthesis and certain medical treatments, but can also cause skin damage. UVC lights emit short wavelengths, effective for disinfecting surfaces and air due to their germicidal properties. Each type serves distinct applications across various industries, including healthcare, cosmetics, and sanitation.</p></p>
