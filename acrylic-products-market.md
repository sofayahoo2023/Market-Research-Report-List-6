<p><ul><li>Extruded Acrylic</li><li>Cast Acrylic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic products market primarily consists of two types: extruded acrylic and cast acrylic. Extruded acrylic is produced by forcing acrylic polymer through a die, resulting in a uniform thickness and lower costs, making it ideal for signage and glazing applications. In contrast, cast acrylic is made by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, leading to higher quality and greater optical clarity, suitable for premium applications like displays, lighting fixtures, and art installations. Both types cater to diverse industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
