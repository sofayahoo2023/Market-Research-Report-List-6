<p><p>The achromats and lens systems market is categorized into three main types: positive achromats, negative achromats, and cylindrical achromats. Positive achromats are designed to minimize chromatic aberration while converging light, often used in imaging systems. Negative achromats diverge light rays, correcting aberrations in systems requiring a wider field of view. Cylindrical achromats focus light into a line rather than a point, making them ideal for applications in laser technology and optical instruments. Each type serves specific optical needs across various industries.</p></p>
