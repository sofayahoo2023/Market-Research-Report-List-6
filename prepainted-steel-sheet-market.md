<p><p>The prepainted steel sheet market is segmented into various types based on the coating materials used. PE prepainted steel features a polyethylene coating, offering good weather resistance and color retention. HDP prepainted steel uses high durability paint, providing enhanced protection against UV rays and corrosion. SMP prepainted steel incorporates a polyester coating, known for its glossy finish and moderate durability. PVDF prepainted steel utilizes polyvinylidene fluoride, delivering superior weather resistance and longevity, making it ideal for demanding environments.</p></p>
