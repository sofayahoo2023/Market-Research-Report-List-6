<p><p>The FTIR Exhaust Gas Analyzer market is divided into two key types: Dilute Measurement Type and Direct Measurement Type. The Dilute Measurement Type involves sampling exhaust gases diluted with a carrier gas, allowing for detection of low concentrations of pollutants, making it suitable for ambient air monitoring. In contrast, the Direct Measurement Type captures the exhaust gases directly from the source, providing real-time analysis of emissions at higher concentrations, ideal for compliance monitoring in industrial applications.</p></p>
