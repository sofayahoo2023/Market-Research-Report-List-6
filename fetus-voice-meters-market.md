<p><p>Fetus voice meters are essential medical devices used to monitor fetal heartbeats during pregnancy. The market is categorized into two main types: Doppler fetus voice meters and passive fetus voice meters. Doppler fetus voice meters utilize ultrasound technology to detect and amplify the fetal heartbeat, providing real-time audio feedback. In contrast, passive fetus voice meters rely on simple mechanical techniques to monitor sounds without electronic amplification, offering a more traditional approach. Both types play crucial roles in prenatal care, enhancing maternal-fetal monitoring.</p></p>
