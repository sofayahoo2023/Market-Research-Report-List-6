<p><p>The screen protector market consists of several types, primarily PET, tempered glass, and other materials. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) protectors offer lightweight, flexible protection against scratches and minor impacts, making them a popular choice for basic safeguarding. Tempered glass screen protectors are thicker and provide superior durability against drops and cracks, often featuring additional coatings for anti-fingerprint and anti-glare properties. Other options may include polyurethane and liquid screen protectors, each offering unique benefits tailored to different user preferences and device requirements.</p></p>
