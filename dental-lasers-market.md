<p><ul><li>Soft Tissue Dental Lasers</li><li>All Tissue Dental Lasers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental lasers market consists of two main types: soft tissue dental lasers and all tissue dental lasers. Soft tissue lasers are designed for procedures involving gums and other soft tissues, allowing for precise incisions, reducing bleeding, and enhancing healing. In contrast, all tissue dental lasers can be used on both hard and soft tissues, making them versatile tools for a wider range of procedures, including cavity preparation and periodontal treatment, thus improving overall dental care efficiency.</p></p>
