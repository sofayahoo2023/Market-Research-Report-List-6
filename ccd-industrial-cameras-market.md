<p><strong>The CCD Industrial Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CCD industrial camera market comprises two main types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture entire images in a single exposure, making them suitable for applications requiring detailed analysis of static scenes, such as quality inspection or measurement. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, ideal for continuous processes like conveyor belt inspection or web applications, where high-speed and high-resolution images of moving objects are essential. Both types serve various industrial needs.</p></p>
