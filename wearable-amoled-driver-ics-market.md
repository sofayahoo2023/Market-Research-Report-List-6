<p><p>The wearable AMOLED driver ICs market is segmented into three main types based on packaging: COG, COF, and COP. </p><p>COG (Chip-on-Glass) integrates the driver IC directly onto the display glass, enhancing compactness and improving performance. COF (Chip-on-Flex) uses a flexible substrate, allowing for versatile designs and better space utilization in wearables. COP (Chip-on-Panel) mounts the IC on the panel, providing effective heat dissipation and durability. Each type serves different design needs, catering to the evolving requirements of wearable technology.</p></p>
