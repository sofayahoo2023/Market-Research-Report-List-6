<p><p>The UV curable inks market encompasses various types, including Free Radical Polymerization, which uses photoinitiators to initiate curing upon exposure to UV light, providing fast drying and high durability. Free Radical Addition Molding involves creating three-dimensional objects through UV curing during the molding process. Cationic Polymerization relies on cationic initiators for curing, offering superior adhesion and chemical resistance. Acid Cure Type inks utilize acid catalysts to enhance curing efficiency, enabling versatile applications in printing and coating industries.</p></p>
