<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Commercial Elliptical Trainer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-drive Ellipticals</li><li>Rear-drive Ellipticals</li><li>Center-drive Ellipticals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial elliptical trainer market features three main types: front-drive, rear-drive, and center-drive ellipticals. Front-drive ellipticals place the flywheel at the front, providing a more intense workout and a longer stride. Rear-drive ellipticals have the flywheel at the back, offering a smoother and more natural motion, which is often quieter. Center-drive ellipticals position the flywheel in the center, creating a compact design that allows for a more balanced and stable workout experience, making them suitable for various gym settings.</p></p>
