<p><strong>The Air Flow Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mass Air Flow Sensors</li><li>Volume Air Flow Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air flow sensors market primarily consists of two types: Mass Air Flow (MAF) sensors and Volume Air Flow (VAF) sensors. MAF sensors measure the mass of air entering an engine, providing critical data for fuel injection control, thereby optimizing performance and emissions. In contrast, VAF sensors quantify the volume of air, commonly utilized in HVAC systems for maintaining indoor air quality and energy efficiency. Both types play crucial roles in automotive and industrial applications, driving advances in technology and efficiency.</p></p>
