<p><p>The arc welding consumables market encompasses several key types, including stick electrodes, solid wires, flux-cored wires, submerged arc welding (SAW) wires, and fluxes. Stick electrodes are versatile and widely used for manual welding. Solid wires provide excellent feedability for MIG welding. Flux-cored wires offer good penetration and usability in various positions. SAW wires enhance productivity in high-volume applications, while fluxes assist in protecting welds from contamination, improving quality. Each type serves specific welding requirements across industries.</p></p>
