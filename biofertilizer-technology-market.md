<p><p>Biofertilizer technology encompasses two primary market types: liquid and carrier-based biofertilizers. Liquid biofertilizers are nutrient-rich solutions containing beneficial microorganisms, easily applied through irrigation systems or spraying, facilitating rapid absorption by plants. Carrier-based biofertilizers, on the other hand, involve microorganisms mixed with a solid carrier material, ensuring sustained release and extended shelf life. Each type offers distinct advantages for enhancing soil fertility and plant growth, catering to diverse agricultural needs and practices.</p></p>
