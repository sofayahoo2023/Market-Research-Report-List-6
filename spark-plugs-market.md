<p><p>The spark plug market is primarily divided into hot and cold spark plugs, each designed for specific engine performance requirements. Hot spark plugs dissipate heat more slowly, making them suitable for high-compression or low-speed engines, where overheating is less likely. In contrast, cold spark plugs dissipate heat rapidly, ideal for high-speed or performance engines that generate more heat. This differentiation influences vehicle performance, fuel efficiency, and emissions, driving demand in specific automotive segments depending on engine design and operating conditions.</p></p>
