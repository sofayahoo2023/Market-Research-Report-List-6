<p><p>The floating oil skimmers market comprises various technologies for efficiently removing oil from water surfaces. Belt oil skimmers utilize a continuous looped belt to capture oil, making them effective for large-scale applications. Disc oil skimmers feature rotating discs that collect oil as they skim the water surface, ideal for smaller spills. Other types include vacuum-based and brush skimmers, each offering unique advantages depending on the spill size and environmental conditions. Together, these solutions enhance oil spill response efforts.</p></p>
