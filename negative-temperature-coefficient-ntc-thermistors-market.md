<p><p>The Negative Temperature Coefficient (NTC) thermistors market comprises several types. Radial type thermistors feature leads extending from a cylindrical body, while diode type integrates temperature sensing within a semiconductor diode structure. Wire bonding type utilizes fine wires to connect the thermistor chip, enhancing thermal response. Film type thermistors are thin and offer rapid temperature changes. SMD (Surface Mount Device) type enables compact circuit designs, whereas wire type is suited for custom applications. Chip in glass type thermistors provide excellent stability and durability in harsh environments.</p></p>
