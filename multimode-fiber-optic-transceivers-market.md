<p><p>The multimode fiber optic transceivers market includes various types, each designed for specific applications. SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable) transceivers are compact and support data rates up to 1 Gbps. SFF (Small Form-factor) is slightly larger but still offers efficient performance. XFP (10 Gigabit Small Form-factor Pluggable) transceivers cater to higher data rates up to 10 Gbps. CXP supports even higher data rates, typically up to 100 Gbps. Others encompass various specialized formats to meet diverse networking needs.</p></p>
