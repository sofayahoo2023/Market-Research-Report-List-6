<p><p>The airway clearance systems market includes several therapeutic types designed to improve pulmonary health. Positive Expiratory Pressure (PEP) devices create back pressure during exhalation, promoting mucus clearance. Oscillatory Positive Expiratory Pressure (OPEP) combines PEP with oscillatory waves for enhanced mucus mobilization. High-Frequency Chest Wall Compression uses rapid vibrations to loosen secretions. Intrapulmonary Percussive Ventilation delivers mini-breaths to promote clearance, while Mechanical Cough Assist mimics natural coughing to expel mucus effectively, benefiting patients with respiratory conditions.</p></p>
