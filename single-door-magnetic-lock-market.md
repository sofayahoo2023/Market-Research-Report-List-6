<p><p>The Single Door Magnetic Lock market encompasses various installation types, primarily categorized into surface-mounted and embedded installations. Surface-mounted locks are affixed externally to the door frame, making them easy to install and ideal for retrofitting existing doors. In contrast, embedded installations involve recessing the magnetic lock into the door frame, offering a more discreet and tamper-resistant solution. Each type serves specific security needs and architectural preferences, contributing to the overall growth and versatility of the magnetic lock market.</p></p>
