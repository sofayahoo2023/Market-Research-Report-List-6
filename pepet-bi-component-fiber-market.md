<p><p>The PE/PET bi-component fiber market is divided into two main types: staple fiber and filament fiber. Staple fiber consists of short lengths that can be spun into yarns, commonly used in textiles, nonwovens, and insulation materials. Filament fiber, on the other hand, is continuous and long, primarily utilized in applications like apparel, industrial fabrics, and geotextiles. Both types leverage the properties of polyethylene and polyethylene terephthalate to enhance performance, durability, and versatility across various industries.</p></p>
