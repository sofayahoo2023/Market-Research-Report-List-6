<p><p>The hair mask market features two primary types: Steam-Free Hair Masks and Thermal Steam Hair Masks. Steam-Free Hair Masks are formulated to deliver deep conditioning without the need for heat, making them convenient for all hair types. These masks often contain rich oils and nutrients that absorb into the hair during application. In contrast, Thermal Steam Hair Masks utilize heat to enhance absorption, promoting effective penetration of active ingredients. These masks work by opening hair cuticles, allowing for intense hydration and repair.</p></p>
