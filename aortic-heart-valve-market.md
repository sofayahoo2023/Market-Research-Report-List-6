<p><p>The aortic heart valve market comprises three primary types: Transcatheter Heart Valves, Tissue Heart Valves, and Mechanical Heart Valves. Transcatheter Heart Valves are minimally invasive options, ideal for high-risk surgical patients requiring valve replacement. Tissue Heart Valves, made from animal or human tissues, naturally integrate into the body but may have a shorter lifespan. Mechanical Heart Valves, constructed from durable materials, offer longevity but necessitate lifelong anticoagulation therapy. Together, these options cater to diverse patient needs and treatment preferences.</p></p>
