<p><p>The Insulation Sealant market includes two primary types: Organic Gum and Inorganic Glue. Organic Gum sealants are derived from natural or synthetic polymers, offering flexibility, adhesion, and resistance to environmental factors. They are commonly used in applications requiring elasticity and durability. Inorganic Glue sealants, made from mineral-based materials, provide high thermal resistance and stability under extreme conditions. They are ideal for high-temperature environments and industrial applications. Both types cater to diverse insulation needs, balancing performance, durability, and environmental suitability.</p></p>
